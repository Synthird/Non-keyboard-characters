Characters not accessable via keyboard

Note:
This page may not contain every non-keyboard character.

Press Ctrl + F to search for a character/symbol.

Arrows

  • Up: ↑
  • Down: ↓
  • Left: ←
  • Right: →
  • Up and down: ↕
  • Left and right: ↔
  • Arrow left circle (Undo & Refresh): ↺
  • Arrow Right circle (Redo): ↻
  • Greater than or equal to: ≥
  • Less than or equal to: ≤

Currency

  • Cent: ¢
  • Euro: €
  • Pounds: £
  • Yan: ¥

Science

  • Approximately: ≈
  • Division: ÷
  • Empty set: ∅
  • Degrees: °
  • Gamma: γ
  • Infinity: ∞
  • Integral: ∫
  • Mu: μ
  • Pi: π
  • Square root/radical: √
  • Sum of: ∑
  • Therefore: ∴
  • Theta: θ
  • Not equal to: ≠

Legal

  • Copyright: ©
  • Trademark: ™
  • Registered: ®

Other

  • Checkmark: ✓
  • Dot (Not filled): ∘
  • Equilibrium: ⇌
  • Heart: ♥
  • Star (Filled): ★
  • Star (Not filled): ☆
